Best of luck to all those competing.

The brief
The brief is simple. We want you to prototype a business idea. The business can be anything, but there are additional prizes for aligning with our sub-themes:
  • How can we create a fairer society?
  • How can we make the web more accessible?
Prizes
There will be prizes for the following:
  • Best technical solution
  • Best contribution to society
  • Best consideration to accessibility
  • Best overall concept
  • Most professionally managed
Step 1: Groupings
Groups can have a maximum of five members. Perhaps you already have a complete group. Perhaps you have a partial group but are missing certain expertise. Or perhaps you are a solo developer looking for a group.

Use the Padlet here to find group members.

Once you have a group, register your group with a team name here
Step 2: Brainstorm
With your group finalised, you next need to figure out what you want to build.
  • What are the strengths and weaknesses of your group?
  • What is achievable in the timeframe?
  • What would your product be?
  • What is your unique selling point?
  • Who would your target market be?
  • How would you monetise your product?
  • What would the roles and responsibilities of each group member be?
Step 3: Build
Nothing to say here, just build your product. Make use of the expertise in the room and the workshops available. Also check the schedule for other activities - Ash intends to destroy you all in the MarioKart tournament.
Step 4: Submit
Each group will present their products on Friday afternoon. Your group will need to submit any slides by Friday @ 12:00 using the link here. We recommend that groups provide a live demo of their products during the presentation. Make sure that your product is hosted online, or that you have it running on a laptop with a HDMI port.
